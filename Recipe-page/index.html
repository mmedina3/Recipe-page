Enchiladas



What are Enchiladas?

Enchiladas are a traditional dish that you will find in Mexico.
They are a blend of tortillas, cheese, salsa and your choice of meat (or vegeterian)
option. I will teach you how to make chicken enchiladas but feel free to replace the chicken with other types of meat or vegetables.
Enchiladas is the first dish that my mom thaught me how to make and today I will share our family recipe with you!

Enchiladas Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • El Pato Sauce- Big can
  • Chicken Breast
  • Mozzarela Shredded Cheese
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Butter/ Margarine

Cook the chicken

Two chicken breasts makes about 10-15 enchiladas.

  1. In a huge pan add water and set time for it to boil.
  2. Add a piece of onion to boil and a little bit of oil.
  3. Once water is boiling add the chicken breasts.
  4. Let chicken boil for about 10 minutes. Use this time to dice the onion and to pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees.
  5. Once the chicken is fully cooked, drain the water out and put the chicken in a large bowl.
  6. Use two forks to shred the chicken, it is easiest to shred when it is hot.
  7. Once the chicken is shredded, set aside.

Photo Credit: Bites of Flavor




Prep the tortillas

We are going to want to heat up tortillas into a soft state so we can fill them up and be able to roll them easily without breaking apart.

  1. Use a flat pan and add a little butter/margarine to melt.
  2. Once the butter has melted heat up a tortilla on low. The tortilla should absorb the butter and soften.
  3. The tortilla will not be completely heated, we just want it to be soft enough to fold and then set it apart on a plate.
  4. Continue the process until you heat up about 15 tortillas. (depending on how much chicken you have)

Photo Credit: All Recipes




Heat up the sauce

We will warm up the suce but keep in mind that we will be dipping the tortillas in the sauce so make sure it is only warm.

  1. On the pan that you used to heat up your torillas add some oil and wait a few seconds for it to heat up.
  2. Add some of the diced onions and stir them around the pan.
  3. Add some garlic and stir with the onions.
  4. Once onions and garlic are lightly browned, add the sauce to the pan and stir.
  5. After a minute turn off the stove. You do not want the sauce to get too hot or you will burn your hands when dipping the tortillas in the pan.

Photo Credit: Land of Noms




Now put it all together!

Time to put all of our ingredients together and make our enchiladas.

  1. Dip a tortilla into the sauce and place the tortilla on a plate.
  2. Once the tortilla is on the plate add some shredded chicken in the center of the tortilla.
  3. Add some mozarella cheese.
  4. Add some diced onions.
  5. Roll the tortilla and add it to a baking pan.
  6. Continue step 1-5 until you have ran out of tortillas/chicken.
  7. Once your baking pan is full of enchiladas add the leftover sauce and spread on top of all the enchiladas.
  8. Spread the mozarella cheese over the top of the enchiladas.
  9. Place the baking pan in the oven and bake for 15 minutes.
  10. Enjoy your Enchiladas!
